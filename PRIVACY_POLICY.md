**Effective Date:** October 10, 2023
**App:** VocabMaster (Telegram Mini App)

## 1. Introduction
We respect your privacy. VocabMaster is designed to collect minimal data. We do not store your personal data on our servers. All learning progress is stored locally on your device and in Telegram's CloudStorage linked to your account.

## 2. Data We Use

### 2.1. Telegram Profile Data
We access basic public profile info provided by the Telegram platform upon launch:
*   First Name
*   Username
*   Profile Picture
*   User ID

**Purpose:** UI personalization and progress synchronization.

### 2.2. Learning Progress
Data regarding learned words, stats, achievements, and virtual currency.

**Storage:**
1.  **Local:** Browser/Phone LocalStorage.
2.  **Telegram CloudStorage:** Encrypted cloud storage accessible only to your account within this bot.

We (the developers) **do not have access** to your CloudStorage.

### 2.3. AI Interaction
When using the "AI Tutor" feature, the word text is sent to the AI provider (Google Gemini API).
*   No personal info is sent.
*   Requests are anonymized.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
We **do not sell, trade, or transfer** your personal information to outside parties.

## 4. Your Rights
*   **Deletion:** You can wipe all data by using the "Reset Progress" button in the app settings or clearing the Telegram bot cache.
*   **Export:** You can backup your data via the Profile settings.
